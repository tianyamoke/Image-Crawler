<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 2em; letter-spacing: 1.5px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">懂哥告诉大家，其实很简单。我们以厂家要求机油添加量为4L的车型来举例子。打开机油加注口并且清理加注口周边的灰尘、垃圾之后，放置漏斗，将机油沿着漏斗壁换换倒入机油加注口。大约倒至瓶内剩余500毫升时，应暂停加注，观察机油尺的刻度，看看机油液面高度距离标准范围还有多少。</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 2em; letter-spacing: 1.5px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 2em; letter-spacing: 1.5px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">

	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">一般来说，此时的机油量已接近或者略微超过最低刻度点。然后按需要补加注一定量的机油，再检查机油尺刻度。当达到机油尺显示的标准范围范围中等偏上的刻度时停止加注机油，拧紧机油加注盖子后点着发动机，运转半分钟后熄火，再静置3-5分钟再次检查机油刻度尺，此时机油尺显示机油量应在最高点和最低点中间附近，即为机油添注完成。</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 2em; letter-spacing: 1.5px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<strong style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;"><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">关于机油放油螺丝和机油滤清器的安装</span></strong></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">

<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 2em; letter-spacing: 1.5px; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">
	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">我们在放出机油之前，会将机油放油螺丝拧下来，以及待放油孔接近没有机油再流出时，将机油滤清器卸下。一般来说，在卸下机油放油螺丝和机油滤清器的步骤没有过于需要注意的点，关键在于它们的安装。我们在正规4S店里面做保养的时候，经常会看到师傅会用力矩扳手对它们进行安装，但在外面保养时，却很少见有师傅会使用它，而是完全凭感觉。放油螺丝的安装必须要按照厂商的要求设定好扭力扳手的扭矩！</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0px; margin-bottom: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; clear: both; min-height: 1em; color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: -apple-system-font, system-ui, &quot;Helvetica Neue&quot;, &quot;PingFang SC&quot;, &quot;Hiragino Sans GB&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei UI&quot;, &quot;Microsoft YaHei&quot;, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; letter-spacing: 0.544px; text-align: justify; white-space: normal; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); line-height: 1.75em; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important;">

	<span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; font-family: Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">如果放油螺丝没有上紧，在我们开车的时候很容易造成机油的渗漏；如果太紧甚至会打崩油底壳的螺纹，造成滑丝。最要紧的是，很多时候滑丝了，车主不在现场发现，操作人员不会告诉到车主，而是会用一颗直径更大的螺丝攻进去，这样做不仅容易导致机油泄露，车主还难以察觉，损害车主的利益。所以懂哥建议大家如果条件允许，爱车保养的时候尽量在旁边看着，操作人员一定会比平时注意很多细节的。如果发现油底壳的螺纹崩了，千万不要听信所谓&ldquo;不影响&rdquo;或者&ldquo;本次保养免费&rdquo;等等话术，坚决要求赔偿油底壳，否则后患无穷！</span></p>
