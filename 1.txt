<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">

</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">哈弗品牌家族化设计语言的演变。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">

</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">哈弗的品牌家族化设计语言，从哈弗CUV的时代就开始了多样化的探索。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">不同于其他品牌的是，哈弗品牌对于家族化造型语言的选择，是一个完全依托于市场的过程。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;"></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">一个典型的例子是，早年间哈弗H5以欧风版和尊贵版两种造型语言投放市场，哈弗H6也提供了运动版以及经典版的前脸造型。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">

<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">市场的选择不会造假，自2013年哈弗品牌正式独立以来，哈弗品牌的家族化造型语言开始固化。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;"></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">在本届德里国际车展上发布的Concept H概念车，就是全新一代哈弗H系列的家族化设计的首款车型。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">从前脸的造型上来看，全新的哈弗Concept H概念车的前脸特征有几分意思。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">就整体的比例以及风格呈现上来看，哈弗Concept H概念车与此前我们所熟悉的哈弗品牌车型保持了一致。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;"></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">比如说，这是现款销售的哈弗H6。</span>

<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">

    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">这是哈弗H4。</span>

<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">可以看到，在现阶段的哈弗H系列品牌造型语言的一大特点在于多边形的大嘴式进气格栅与切角式大灯。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">二者构成了视觉重心相对靠下的侵略感设计。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">同样的，在哈弗Concept H概念车上，这种下沉的侵略感风格得以保留。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;"></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">而相比于现款的哈弗H系列造型语言的升级在于，通过进气格栅横向尺寸的拓展，以及缩小大灯在车头正面投影中的比例，实现了大灯与进气格栅的融合，</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">弱化了大灯在车头部分的视觉重心作用，实现了车头横向宽度的拓展。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">这一家族化造型语言演变的思路，和奥迪最新一代的家族化演变思路是一致的。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">而奥迪的家族化演变，是这样的。</span>
</p>

    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">在强化车头横向宽度的同时，哈弗Concept H概念车又以两侧的LED日间行车灯所形成的纵向特征，以及两侧进气口尺寸的加大，构成了纵向的特征，对横线感强化的大灯等区域进行了平衡。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;"></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">于是，全新的哈弗Concept H概念车的车头造型，也就被牢牢的限制在了一个可控的范围内，没有过多的向两侧扩散开。</span><br/>

<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">至于前脸的一些细节设计，包括点阵式的进气格栅，以及两侧进气口的横向特征，都将这辆车科技感和品质感的一面进行了呈现。</span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">尤其是前保险杠两侧进气口的横向特征，是绝对要好评的。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">

</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;"></span><span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">于是，相较于现阶段的哈弗H系列车型的尾部，全新的哈弗Concept H概念车要稳重了一些。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">没错，稳重，或者说紧致，是哈弗Concept H概念车所要强调的一个设计主旨。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">

    <span style="margin: 0px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; font-size: 15px; letter-spacing: 2px;">就整体上而言，哈弗Concept H概念车要比现在任何的一款哈弗H系列车型都来得更为紧致。</span>
</p>
<p style="margin: 15px 16px; padding: 0px; max-width: 100%; box-sizing: border-box !important; overflow-wrap: break-word !important; clear: both; min-height: 1em; line-height: 1.75em;">
